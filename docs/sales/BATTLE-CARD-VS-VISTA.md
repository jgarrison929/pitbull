## Where We Win

- **Modern stack.** Web-based, mobile-friendly, dark mode, keyboard shortcuts. Your people will actually use it instead of working around it.
- **All-in-one.** Vista does money. That's it. You still need Procore or Viewpoint Team for PM. Pitbull does both.
- **AI understands your projects.** Cost-to-complete predictions, invoice matching, smart field suggestions. Vista will never have this. Trimble isn't investing.
- **Deploy in days.** CSV import your chart of accounts, projects, vendors, employees. Run parallel for a month. Cut over. No 12-month implementation with $200K in consulting fees.
- **Actually gets updates.** We ship features weekly. Vista gets tax tables annually.
- **Predictive, not reactive.** Vista tells you what happened. Pitbull tells you what's going to happen.

## Where They Win

- **20+ years of accounting depth.** Vista handles edge cases in construction accounting that take years to discover. Retainage releases, JC cost type overrides, complex multi-entity allocations. Our financial modules are newer and may not cover every edge case yet.
- **Existing data.** A Vista shop has 10-20 years of historical job cost data. Migration is real work.
- **Ecosystem.** Payroll processors, insurance auditors, CPAs, and sureties know how to read Vista reports. New report formats mean retraining external partners.
- **"Nobody gets fired for keeping Vista."** Switching ERP is scary. The devil you know vs the devil you don't.

## Objection Handling

**"We've been on Vista for 15 years. Migration is too risky."**
> You don't migrate day one. You start fresh on new projects, run parallel for a month, and cut over when you're comfortable. We don't touch your historical data until you're ready. And honestly, when's the last time Vista did something new for you?

**"Our controller knows Vista inside and out."**
> That's expertise, and it transfers. The accounting concepts are the same. GL, JC, AP, AR, billing. The difference is your controller will spend less time fighting the software and more time analyzing the numbers. And the AI handles the grunt work they're doing manually today.

**"Trimble will modernize Vista eventually."**
> They bought Viewpoint in 2018. It's 2026. What have they shipped? Tax table updates. They're not investing in Vista. They're milking it. Your anchor is our opportunity.

**"Can you handle our multi-company setup?"**
