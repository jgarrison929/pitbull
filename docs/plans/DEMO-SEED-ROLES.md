## Implementation Notes

### Password
All demo accounts: `PitbullDemo2026!` (same as current demo user)

### Role-Based Dashboard
Each user should land on a dashboard tailored to their role:
- **C-Suite:** KPIs, portfolio health, financial summary, AI morning briefing
- **VP/Director:** Department-specific metrics, team performance, pending approvals
- **Manager:** Operational queues, approval workflows, team workload
- **Staff/Clerk:** Task-focused views, data entry queues, my items
- **Project Leadership:** Project portfolio, margin analysis, risk heatmap
- **Project Management:** Schedule, RFIs, submittals, daily reports, cost tracking
- **Field:** Mobile-first, daily report, time entry, punch list, photos
- **Estimating:** Bid pipeline, takeoff workspace, historical cost data

### What Each Role "Replaces"
The pitch: *"You're paying for Vista + Procore + P6 + InEight + SharePoint + Excel + 5 other tools. Log in as your role and see it all in one place."*

### Multi-Company
Seed users across the demo company structure:
- **Summit Builders Group** (parent) — C-suite, corporate staff
- **Summit Commercial Construction** — PM team, field team
- **Pacific Civil** — Estimating, field engineers
- **Pacific Mechanical** — Specialty trades

### Realistic Data
Each role should have relevant data waiting:
- CFO: 6 months of financials, WIP schedule, aging reports
- PM: 3 active projects with RFIs, submittals, schedule
- Estimator: 2 bids in progress, 1 won, 1 lost
- AP Clerk: 15 invoices to process, 5 needing approval
- Field Engineer: Today's daily report, 3 open punch items
